About Our Team

We're a group of passionate developers who believe that creativity shouldn't be rushed. Our "lazy" approach means we take the time to get things right, even if it means moving at our own pace.

The local Singinx Studio circle was founded in 2021 by two Touhou fans dreaming of creating something similar. The first two founders were Miranda Karlene and Singinx himself. The team composition has been constantly changing, with people coming and going like the water cycle in nature, but the main core is now before you.

The nickname Singinx can be broken down as Sin and Singing + a very youthful sound with "inx", which means "The Singing Sinner". The entire team works on pure enthusiasm for fun, which is important.

Player Ideas Welcome!

We're always interested in hearing ideas from our players! If you have a cool concept, mechanic, or story idea, feel free to share it with us. While we can't promise anything, with a certain chance your idea might appear in our future games or updates.

Just don't ask us to make a visual novel - Venom Shadow might have a panic attack.

Want to publish your game with us?

Interested individuals have access to an agreement whereby the author of their game can request help (in everything except drawing graphics - we can't do that ourselves) and publish the game under our name.

We provide programming, sound design, testing, and community management support. You bring the idea and vision!

We're looking for an artist!

The group is actively looking for an artist for our projects. If you can draw character sprites, backgrounds, or UI elements and want to collaborate on game development, we'd love to hear from you!
